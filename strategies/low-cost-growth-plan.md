# EventShare: Low-Cost Growth Marketing Plan

*Created: 2026-01-30*
*Focus: Maximaal bereik, minimale kosten*

---

## Executive Summary

Dit plan focust op **gratis en goedkope kanalen** om EventShare te laten groeien zonder groot marketingbudget. De strategie combineert:
- Product-led growth (PLG)
- Content marketing & SEO
- Community building
- Strategische partnerships
- Viral loops

**Doel:** 100 betaalde events/maand binnen 6 maanden met <€500/maand marketingkosten.

---

## 1. Product-Led Growth (GRATIS)

### 1.1 Viral Loop in Product
**Wat:** Elke EventShare event promoot zichzelf automatisch.

**Implementatie:**
- "Powered by EventShare" watermark op slideshow (subtiel, professioneel)
- QR code pagina toont "Get EventShare for your event" link
- Na event: email naar organizer met "Share your success" template
- Download pagina toont "Create your own event" CTA

**Cost:** €0 (development time only)
**Impact:** Elke event = potentiële nieuwe klant

### 1.2 Freemium Optimalisatie
**Huidige:** Eerste 10 foto's gratis

**Verbeteringen:**
- Maak gratis tier genereuzer: 25 foto's of 1 uur event
- Voeg "Upgrade" prompt toe bij limiet naderen
- Toon wat premium features zijn (geen watermark, HD download, analytics)
- Exit-intent popup: "Get 50% off your first event"

### 1.3 Referral Programma
**Structuur:**
- Organizer krijgt: €10 korting voor elke referral die betaalt
- Nieuwe klant krijgt: €10 korting op eerste event
- Na 3 referrals: 1 gratis event

**Implementatie:** Simpele referral link per account

## 3. Social Media (GRATIS)

### 3.1 LinkedIn Strategy (Primary)
**Why LinkedIn:** Decision makers (event planners, marketing managers) zijn actief.

**Posting cadence:** 5x/week
- Maandag: Industry insight/statistic
- Dinsdag: How-to tip
- Woensdag: Customer success/testimonial
- Donderdag: Behind-the-scenes/product update
- Vrijdag: Engaging question/poll

**Engagement tactics:**
- Comment op event industry posts (30 min/dag)
- Join en participeer in: Event Planners Network, Corporate Events Professionals
- Connect met event coordinators, HR managers, marketing leads
- DM strategie: Personalized outreach na engagement

### 3.2 Instagram Strategy (Secondary)
**Content:**
- Reels: Quick tips, event photo inspiration, before/after
- Stories: Polls, Q&A, user-generated content
- Feed: Beautiful event photo galleries, testimonials

**Hashtag strategy:**
#eventphotography #corporateevents #weddingplanning #eventplanner #eventtech #conferencelife

### 3.3 TikTok (Experimental)
- Target: Young event planners, wedding planners
- Content: "POV: collecting photos at your event", quick tutorials
- Low effort: Repurpose Instagram Reels

## 4. Community & Partnerships (GRATIS)

### 4.1 Reddit & Forums
**Target subreddits:**
- r/eventplanning (48k members)
- r/weddingplanning (1.3M members)
- r/conferencing
- r/marketing

**Approach:**
- Be helpful first, mention product only when relevant
- Answer questions about event photo collection
- Create valuable posts (guides, tips)
- AMA: "I built an event photo sharing tool, AMA"

### 4.2 Quora
**Target questions:**
- "How can I collect photos from wedding guests?"
- "Best way to share photos at corporate events"
- "How to create a live photo slideshow"

**Write detailed, helpful answers with subtle product mention.**

### 4.3 Strategic Partnerships

**Tier 1: Event Venues (Free cross-promo)**
- Offer free EventShare for events at partner venues
- Venue mentions EventShare in packages
- Co-branded QR codes

**Tier 2: Wedding Vendors**
- Partner met wedding planners, photographers
- Zij promoten EventShare, krijgen affiliate commission (10%)
- "Recommended by [Photographer Name]"

**Tier 3: Event Software Integration**
- Integrate met: Eventbrite, Splash, Bizzabo
- "Works with EventShare" badge
- Featured in their marketplace

### 4.4 Event Industry Communities
- Join: MPI (Meeting Professionals International)
- Attend: Local event planner meetups (free)
- Sponsor: Small event industry podcasts (€50-100/episode)

## 5. Email Marketing (GRATIS)

### 5.1 Lead Capture
- Exit-intent popup: "Get our Event Photo Collection Guide"
- Blog sidebar: Newsletter signup
- Footer CTA: "Free event planning tips"

### 5.2 Email Sequences

**Welcome Sequence (5 emails):**
1. Day 0: Welcome + quick start guide
2. Day 2: "3 tips for more guest photos"
3. Day 5: Case study / success story
4. Day 8: Feature highlight + trial extension offer
5. Day 14: "Ready to try EventShare?"

**Post-Event Sequence:**
1. Immediately: "Your event photos are ready!"
2. Day 1: "How did EventShare help?"
3. Day 3: "Share your experience" + review request
4. Day 7: "Your next event?" + discount offer

### 5.3 Cold Email (Careful/Low Volume)
- Target: Event agencies, corporate event teams
- Use: Hunter.io (free tier), LinkedIn for email finding
- Volume: Max 20 personalized emails/week
- Focus: Value first, not sales pitch

## 7. Paid Marketing (MINIMAL BUDGET)

### 7.1 Google Ads - Bottom Funnel Only (€200/maand max)
**Target:** High-intent keywords only
- "event photo sharing app"
- "wedding photo qr code"
- "corporate event photo collection tool"

**Avoid:** Expensive broad keywords

### 7.2 Retargeting (€100/maand max)
- Pixel: Website visitors
- Audience: Visited pricing page, didn't convert
- Ad: "Still thinking? Get 20% off"

### 7.3 LinkedIn Ads (€100/maand test)
- Format: Single image, direct to trial
- Targeting: Job title = Event Coordinator/Manager
- Test: 2 weeks, measure CAC

# EventShare Corporate Events Marketing Strategy

*Created: 2026-01-29*

## Strategic Rationale

Based on competitor analysis, the wedding market is saturated while corporate events are underserved. This strategy focuses on capturing the corporate/conference segment.

## Target Personas

### 1. Event Coordinator (Primary)
- **Role:** Plans corporate events, team buildings, conferences
- **Pain points:**
  - Need professional-looking photo collection
  - Limited time for setup
  - Boss wants engagement metrics
- **Decision factors:** Ease of use, professional appearance, cost justification

### 2. Marketing Manager (Secondary)
- **Role:** Runs company events, trade shows
- **Pain points:**
  - Needs content for social media/internal comms
  - Wants employee engagement
  - Time-sensitive deliverables
- **Decision factors:** Quality output, speed, brand alignment

### 3. Conference Organizer (Tertiary)
- **Role:** Plans multi-day conferences, seminars
- **Pain points:**
  - Large attendee numbers
  - Multiple sessions/rooms
  - Real-time engagement
- **Decision factors:** Scalability, reliability, live display

## Value Propositions by Persona

### For Event Coordinators
> "Capture every moment of your corporate event. Set up in 2 minutes, get photos from all attendees, display live on any screen. No app downloads required."

### For Marketing Managers
> "Turn your events into content goldmines. Collect authentic photos from attendees, curate the best, export for your next campaign."

### For Conference Organizers
> "Engage 500 attendees as easily as 50. QR code on every badge, live slideshow on main stage, memories delivered instantly."

## Channel Strategy

### 1. LinkedIn (Primary)
- Target: Event professionals, HR, Marketing managers
- Content: Tips, case studies, industry stats
- Ads: Retargeting + lookalike audiences

### 2. Google Ads (Secondary)
- Keywords: "corporate event photos", "conference photo sharing", "team building photos"
- Focus on high-intent search terms

### 3. Event Industry Publications
- Guest posts on event planning blogs
- Advertise in event management newsletters

### 4. Direct Outreach
- Email event agencies
- Partner with venue managers

## Messaging Framework

### Headline Options
1. "Event photos from every guest. Zero app downloads."
2. "Your corporate event, captured by everyone who was there."
3. "The 2-minute setup that captures 1000 memories."

### Key Messages
1. **Simplicity:** "Set up in 2 minutes with just a QR code"
2. **Engagement:** "Get photos from guests who'd never think to share"
3. **Display:** "Turn any TV into a live photo wall"
4. **Value:** "$49 for unlimited event photos"

### Objection Handlers
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We just use a shared album" | "And how many photos do you actually get? EventShare makes it effortless." |
| "It's just one event" | "One event, hundreds of authentic photos, permanent memories." |
| "We have a photographer" | "Perfect - EventShare captures what the photographer misses: candid moments from every angle." |

## Metrics & KPIs

- **Awareness:** LinkedIn impressions, website traffic
- **Consideration:** Trial signups (free 10 photos)
- **Conversion:** Paid event purchases
- **Target:** 10 new paid events/month within 6 months

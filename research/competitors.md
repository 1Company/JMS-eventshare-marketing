# EventShare Competitor Analysis

*Last updated: 2026-01-28*

## Market Overview

The event photo sharing market is active with several players. Most focus on weddings, but corporate events are underserved.

## Direct Competitors

### 1. GuestCam (guestcam.co)
- **Features:** QR code upload, live slideshows, ZIP download, collaboration
- **Unique:** Upload reminders for guests
- **Pricing:** Unknown (need to check)

### 2. Kululu (kululu.com)
- **Features:** QR code, photo wall/digital guestbook, photographer integration
- **Unique:** Free tier available, global
- **Position:** Budget-friendly option

### 3. WedTrove
- **Features:** Unlimited galleries, full-resolution uploads, real-time display
- **Unique:** Fast concurrent uploads, template themes
- **Storage:** 1 year cloud storage

### 4. Wedibox (wedibox.com)
- **Features:** QR code, collective digital album
- **Focus:** Weddings and events

### 5. DropEvent (dropevent.com)
- **Features:** Single link upload/download
- **Markets:** Weddings, corporate, trade shows, contests
- **Unique:** Large-scale collection from strangers

### 6. GuestPix (guestpix.com)
- **Features:** QR code platform, guestbook
- **Markets:** Weddings, business events, memorials

### 7. ReplayMyDay (replaymyday.com)
- **Features:** Live slideshows, QR code, AI-enabled sharing
- **Unique:** 30-second setup
- **Markets:** Broad (weddings to corporate)

### 8. MagicAlbum (magicalbum.link)
- Newer/smaller player
- QR code + link sharing

## EventShare Differentiators

### Current Advantages
- ✅ TV slideshow focus (live display)
- ✅ Trial mode (first 10 photos free)
- ✅ Simple pricing ($49 for 7 days)
- ✅ Organizer curation tools

### Potential Gaps to Fill
- ❓ Upload reminders (GuestCam has this)
- ❓ Photographer integration
- ❓ Template themes
- ❓ Longer storage options
- ❓ AI features (face recognition for sharing)

## Target Market Analysis

### Underserved Segments
1. **Corporate events** - Most competitors focus on weddings
2. **Recurring events** - Subscription model opportunity
3. **Large conferences** - Need scalability messaging

### Well-served Segments
- Weddings (very competitive)
- Birthday parties

## Recommendations

1. **Positioning:** Focus on corporate/conference market initially
2. **Differentiation:** Emphasize TV slideshow quality and simplicity
3. **Pricing:** Consider monthly subscription for repeat event organizers
4. **Feature priority:** Upload reminders, longer storage

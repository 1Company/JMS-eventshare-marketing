## Template 4: After Someone Signs Up (Nurture)

**Subject:** Getting the most out of EventShare at your event

Hi [Name],

Thanks for creating your event on EventShare! Here are 3 quick tips to maximize photo collection:

**1. Display the QR code prominently**
Put it on table tents, screens, or even name badges. The more visible, the more uploads.

**2. Announce it early**
Tell attendees: "Your photos will appear live on the big screen!" This gets people excited to participate.

**3. Turn on the slideshow**
When people see their photo appear, they share more. It creates a fun feedback loop.

Questions? Just reply to this email.

Good luck with your event!
[Your name]

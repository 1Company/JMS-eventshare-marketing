## Product Overview

**EventShare** is a browser-based event photo sharing application.
- Guests upload photos from any device
- Organizers curate the best shots
- Published photos appear on a live TV slideshow

**Pricing:** $49 for 7-day unlimited uploads (first 10 photos free trial)

## Target Markets

### Primary
- Wedding planners & couples
- Corporate event organizers
- Conference & seminar hosts
- Birthday/anniversary party planners

### Secondary
- Photography studios (upsell service)
- Venue owners (partner opportunity)
- Event agencies
